FAQ

Questions, answered

What exactly is Couponaut?
Couponaut is an AI-powered search engine for coupons and discounts. You ask in plain language and we return the best working codes, validated and ranked — instead of a wall of expired ones.
Is there a free plan?
Yes — you get 10 searches every day for free, no credit card required. Upgrade any time for more searches and an ad-free experience.
Do the coupons actually work?
Codes are auto-validated and scored for reliability before you ever see them. Offers can still expire, so we always show the freshest, best-ranked deals first.
How is it so fast?
A background engine continuously discovers, validates and indexes deals into Meilisearch with a Redis cache. Search reads pre-indexed data and never scrapes while you wait — so results return in under 200ms.
I'm a brand — can I partner with you?
Absolutely. We integrate with major affiliate networks (Awin, CJ, Impact, ShareASale) and direct merchant feeds. Get in touch and we'll get your offers in front of high-intent shoppers.
